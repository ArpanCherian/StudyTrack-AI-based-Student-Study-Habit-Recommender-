About the Project

StudyTrack AI Recommender is designed to support students in their academic journey by identifying study patterns and suggesting strategies tailored to individual needs. Using advanced machine learning, it adapts to your progress and provides real-time recommendations that help you:

  • Optimize your daily and weekly study schedules.
  • Track focus levels and recommend breaks for better productivity.
  • Suggest personalized learning techniques (flashcards, summaries, practice tests).
  • Monitor progress and highlight areas needing improvement.

How It Helps Students

Personalized Plans

AI generates study schedules that match your learning style and pace.

Better Focus

Track study patterns and receive reminders for breaks to avoid burnout.

Progress Insights

Visual reports on your study habits and areas for improvement.

Motivation & Support

Stay motivated with tips, reminders, and encouragement tailored to your journey.
